Heckler & Koch MP5

Sub-Machine Gun

The classic modern Sub Machine Gun star of movies like 'Die Hard'. The real MP5 (German: Maschinenpistole 5) is a 9mm submachine gun, developed in the 1960s by a team of engineers from the German small arms manufacturer Heckler & Koch GmbH (H&K). There are over 100 variants of the MP5. The MP5 is one of the most widely used submachine guns in the world, having been adopted by 40 nations and numerous military, law enforcement, intelligence, and security organisations. It is widely used by SWAT teams in North America.
